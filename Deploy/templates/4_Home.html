Detection
Intrusion

Intrusion detection attacks refer to the various tactics employed by malicious actors to circumvent or undermine intrusion detection systems (IDS). These attacks are particularly insidious because they aim to evade the watchful eyes of security mechanisms and carry out unauthorized activities on a network or system without detection. Evasion attacks, one prominent category of intrusion detection attacks, are engineered to deceive or overload the IDS, leading it to overlook potentially harmful activities.
